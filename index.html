NFC Tags Introduce a fun and exciting new way for visitors to tour Art Galleries, Museums or Shows and get the 'Inside Scoop'. 


Art Lovers. Your next great discovery may be just one click away.

Now, with the ArtWorks4Me™ app, visitors to Art Galleries, museums or shows can have all the facts they need in the palm of their hand. Just swipe an NFC Tag attached to each artwork. The information is downloaded and can be recalled at any time.

NFC Tags come in all shapes & sizes

NFC TAG REQUIREMENTS

Apple requires an iOS 7 or higher device as well as at least  iOS 11. Many Android devices already support NFC technology. With the arrival of iOS 13 over 500 million Apple devices can now read NFC tags.This is fundamentally going to create a digital marketing revolution.

HOW DOES IT ALL WORK?

NFC (Near Frequency Communication) tags allow you to simply scan an item with your phone (much like Apple Pay) and instead of money changing hands the app displays a website linked to that tag.

SCAN NFC Tags

Using your iPhone scan the NFC Tag associated with each artwork.

GET THE 'INSIDE SCOOP'

The ‘inside scoop’ is downloaded and appears on your iPhone. Learn more and see other works by the same artist.

SAVE TAGGED ARTWORKS

All tagged artworks are conveniently saved so that you can recall them later.

RATE FAVOURITE ARTWORKS

Rate your favorite artworks for your next visit to the gallery. Plan your return visit to this gallery with turn by turn directions.
